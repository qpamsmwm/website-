About Us

Motorstar Philippines is a trusted name in the motorcycle industry, assembling high-quality motorcycles and scooters in the Philippines with top-tier components primarily sourced from China. Established in February 2002, it is exclusively assembled and distributed by Eastworld Motor Industries Corporation (EMIC), ensuring excellent craftsmanship and service. Committed to quality and customer satisfaction, Motorstar provides high-performance motorcycles with reliable after-sales support. As a member of the Chamber of Assemblers and Manufacturers of Motorcycles of the Philippines (CHAMMP), recognized by the Board of Investments, the company actively contributes to industry growth and innovation.

Our Mission

Our mission is to be the leading Motorcycle financing company, that best address China brand to all kind of clients.
